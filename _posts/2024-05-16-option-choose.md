# How to set up option strategy
   Stock is 1-D function, direction
   Option is 3-D function, D-T-V
   
   Direction(Delta)(gamma) - D 
   Time(Theta) - T
   Volatility(Vega) - V

1. expiration date
    - farther out the expiration date, more expensive the option will be.
